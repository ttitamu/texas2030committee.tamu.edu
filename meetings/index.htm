Public Meetings

All meetings are open to anyone wishing to attend and listen. At these meetings, the 2030 Committee will receive briefings from the needs assessment team on the status of their efforts and review and finalize the draft report.

The Committee held its initial organizational meeting and team briefing in Austin on June 24, 2008. At the Committee's second meeting on July 24, 2008, the Committee received the needs assessment team's proposed draft report outline.
