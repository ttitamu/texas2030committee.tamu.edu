2009 Report

The 2030 Committee Texas Transportation Needs Report provides a comprehensive analysis of estimated transportation needs, associated costs in 2008 dollars and resulting benefits from highway maintenance (pavements and bridges), urban mobility and rural mobility and safety. The report was adopted by the Texas Transportation Commission on February 26, 2009.
